FitPlan AI - Milestone 1
This Space contains the Milestone 1 implementation of the FitPlan AI project: a fitness profile collection form with BMI calculator.

Features
User-friendly fitness profile form
BMI calculation with proper unit conversion
Input validation
Real-time results display
How to use
Fill in your personal information (name, height, weight, age)
Select your fitness goals and preferences
Choose available equipment
Click "Calculate BMI & Generate Profile"
View your BMI results and complete profile summary
About
This is the first milestone of the FitPlan AI project, focusing on front-end development and BMI calculation logic before AI model integration.
